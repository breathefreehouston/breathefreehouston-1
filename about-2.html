About Me

My name is Indrani Maitra, and I am an eighth-grader at St. John’s School in Houston, Texas. Our curriculum includes a Civics course where we have learned about the principles underlying our nation’s government, the role of citizens, and most recently, how we as students can be civically engaged in our society. Our final project is the Capstone Project, which requires us to identify and research a local issue and write to a key figure advocating for the issue with a proposed solution. The main focus of my project is the great risk that the proposed I-45 expansion will put on the respiratory health of children attending schools near the highway. This website is a supporting feature of my project (along with my twitter feed, which highlights the problem I chose for my Capstone Project). This website widens the scope and provides resources for Houston children suffering with asthma.
